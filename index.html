FanGramDesign directions · v1

Four ways FanGram could look and feel.

Every direction below is the full site: the same sections as the current dev build (hero, players, pre-orders, process, authenticity, footer), rebuilt brick by brick in a distinct design language. Open each one and scroll.

01 Open direction

Icons, Elevated

Cinematic editorial. Film-poster light, a serif voice, gold restraint. Instant credibility, a luxury athlete brand.
02 Open direction

Aura

The future of fandom. Glass, glow and orbiting light. Status you can see. The natural home for points and play.
03 Open direction

The Vault

Museum-grade. Relics under gallery light, numbered plaques, gold on warm black. Authenticity as the whole experience.
04 Open direction

Live

Kinetic match-day energy. Heavy type, bold color, tickers and stats. Young, loud, and still premium.

How to read these: the lit silhouettes and monogram cards are stand-ins for licensed player photography and product shots. The decision here is the design language: type, color, motion, and the feeling each direction creates. Same content, four worlds.
